# Website Conversion Audit Rubric

Score the website out of 100.

## 1. 3-Second Clarity — 15 points

- 15: Offer, buyer, outcome, credibility, and next step are instantly clear.
- 10: Mostly clear but one key element is vague.
- 5: Visitor can infer the offer after reading more.
- 0: Visitor cannot quickly understand what is being sold.

## 2. Hero Section — 10 points

- 10: Strong headline, outcome subheadline, visual, CTA, and trust signal.
- 7: Good hero but missing one major element.
- 4: Hero is visually present but weak or generic.
- 0: Hero fails to explain the offer.

## 3. CTA Specificity — 10 points

- 10: CTA is specific, action-oriented, and repeated at useful points.
- 7: CTA is acceptable but generic or inconsistently placed.
- 4: CTA exists but does not explain the next step.
- 0: CTA is missing or confusing.

## 4. Trust and Proof — 15 points

- 15: Proof is specific, real, early, repeated, and relevant.
- 10: Some useful proof exists but could be stronger or earlier.
- 5: Proof is weak, generic, or buried.
- 0: No proof or unsupported claims dominate.

## 5. Buyer Education — 10 points

- 10: Page explains what it is, how it works, why it matters, and what happens next.
- 7: Good explanation but misses one key buyer question.
- 4: Some explanation but still leaves major uncertainty.
- 0: Page assumes the visitor already understands everything.

## 6. Friction Removal — 10 points

- 10: Flow is focused, simple, and low-friction.
- 7: Minor friction exists but does not break the journey.
- 4: Multiple friction points slow conversion.
- 0: Visitor is forced to hunt for key information.

## 7. Mobile-First UX — 10 points

- 10: Mobile experience is clear, readable, fast, and action-ready.
- 7: Mostly mobile-friendly with minor issues.
- 4: Mobile experience is usable but cramped or unclear.
- 0: Mobile experience likely blocks conversion.

## 8. Forms / Lead Capture — 5 points

- 5: Form is short, clear, and explains next step.
- 3: Form works but has friction.
- 1: Form asks too much or lacks context.
- 0: Form is missing, broken, or confusing.

## 9. Pricing / Offer Clarity — 5 points

- 5: Pricing or offer mechanics are clear.
- 3: Some direction exists but buyer still has questions.
- 1: Pricing/offer is vague.
- 0: Pricing/offer clarity is absent.

## 10. Visual Explanation — 5 points

- 5: Visuals explain the product/service/workflow.
- 3: Visuals support the page but could be clearer.
- 1: Visuals are decorative.
- 0: No useful visuals.

## 11. Technical Basics — 5 points

- 5: SEO metadata, OG metadata, semantic HTML, image basics, and performance basics are handled.
- 3: Some technical basics are handled.
- 1: Several technical basics are missing.
- 0: Technical setup visibly hurts discoverability or conversion.
