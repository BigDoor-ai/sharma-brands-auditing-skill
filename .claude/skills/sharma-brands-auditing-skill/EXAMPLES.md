# Examples

## Example 1: Weak SaaS hero

### Current

**Headline:** The future of sales automation is here.  
**CTA:** Learn More

### Issue

The headline is vague. The buyer does not know what the product does, who it is for, or what outcome it creates.

### Better

**Headline:** Automate WhatsApp follow-ups for every expo lead.  
**Subheadline:** Capture visitor cards, notes, and follow-up reminders in one shared sheet before your sales team forgets who was serious.  
**CTA:** Start Expo Lead Setup

## Example 2: Weak proof

### Current

> Trusted by many businesses.

### Issue

Generic and unsupported.

### Better

> [Add verified client logo]  
> [Add customer testimonial about setup speed, lead quality, or team adoption]
