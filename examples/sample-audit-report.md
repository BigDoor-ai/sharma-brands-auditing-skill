# Sample Website Conversion Audit

## Overall Score

**64/100**

## Executive Summary

The page has a visually polished layout, but the hero does not clearly explain who the product is for or what outcome it creates. The CTA is vague, proof appears too late, and pricing/offer clarity is weak. The page needs stronger 3-second clarity, earlier trust signals, and more specific next-step copy.

## 3-Second Clarity Test

### What a cold visitor likely understands

- The company offers some kind of AI or automation service.
- The page wants the visitor to book or contact the company.

### What is unclear

- The exact buyer.
- The specific workflow or product.
- The business outcome.
- Why this company is credible.
- What happens after clicking the CTA.

### Suggested improved hero copy

**Headline:**  
Automate WhatsApp follow-ups for every expo lead.

**Subheadline:**  
Capture visiting cards, conversation notes, voice messages, and follow-up reminders in one shared sheet during the exhibition.

**Primary CTA:**  
Start Expo Lead Setup

## Priority Fixes

### Fix Now

- Rewrite hero headline and subheadline.
- Replace vague CTA with a specific action.
- Move trust proof near the top.
- Add what happens after form submission.

### Fix Next

- Add workflow diagram.
- Add 6-8 objection-handling FAQs.
- Add pricing/package clarity.

### Test Later

- Test hero angle: speed vs. lead quality.
- Test CTA: “Start Setup” vs. “Book Demo”.
